Research

I am a PhD candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I am advised by Prof. Venugopal Veeravalli. Before that, I graduated with a Diploma (BSc+MEng) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Patras, at which I worked with Prof. George Moustakides.

My research interests broadly encompass machine learning, with a current focus on practical non-stationary reinforcement learning and large language model reasoning. I have also worked on imitation learning, inverse reinforcement learning and autonomous driving.
